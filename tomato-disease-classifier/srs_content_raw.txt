Preface
 
 This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) aims to outline the requirements and specifications for the Smart Tomato Disease Classification System. It is intended for the stakeholders of the project, including agricultural users (farmers/specialists) and the software/AI development team (AI Engineers, Software Engineers, and Project Managers). This document serves as the foundation for the system's architecture, design, and subsequent development phases.
Revision History


Introduction
2.1 Overview
In commercial tomato cultivation and greenhouse systems, plant diseases are a primary cause of crop damage and significant revenue loss. Detecting plant diseases in the early stages usually requires agricultural academics or skilled farmers to observe leaf abnormalities. If diagnosis is delayed, the disease can spread to other plants and become difficult to control. Therefore, this project proposes an AI-assisted plant disease diagnostic system to help farmers scan tomato leaves to identify diseases quickly and accurately, allowing for timely intervention. 2.2 The Objective
To develop an AI mechanism that can accurately classify the type of disease on tomato leaves or identify healthy leaves from photographs.
To develop a website that helps farmers evaluate tomato plant health and record outbreak data into a farm management system.

2.3 Benefits
Yield Protection: Farmers can immediately identify and remove diseased plants before the disease spreads to others.
Reduced Chemical and Management Costs: Knowing the exact disease allows for the targeted use of biopesticides or chemicals, eliminating unnecessary blanket spraying.

3.2 System Environments
3.2.1 System Architectures

The Smart Tomato Disease Classification System consists of two main modules: 
1) System Module: Handles the user interface, allowing farmers to upload images and view real-time diagnostic results. 
2) AI Module: Receives the uploaded image, performs feature extraction using EfficientNet-B3, and returns the classification result and confidence score.

EfficientNet-B3
A convolutional neural network (CNN) architecture used by the AI model for feature extraction and image classification.

A percentage value generated by the AI indicating its level of certainty in the detected disease classification.
10 Classes
The target categorization for the AI, consisting of 9 specific diseases (e.g., Bacterial Spot, Early Blight) and 1 Healthy state.

The AI model shall extract features from lesions, colors, and abnormalities on the leaf using a CNN (EfficientNet-B3).
AI-REQ-02
The AI model shall classify the image into one of 10 designated classes (9 diseases, 1 healthy).

Post-conditions:

Flow of Events::
 S1. The farmer selects the image upload area on the website.

S2. The farmer uploads a photograph of a tomato leaf.

S3. The system sends the image to the AI Module.

S4. The AI Model extracts features and classifies the image into one of 10 classes.

S5. The system displays the Result (e.g., "Bacterial Spot Detected") and the Confidence Score (e.g., "89% Accuracy") on the screen.
Alternative of Events::
 [A1] In the case that the farmer wants to change the image, the farmer clicks the "Cancel" or "Upload New Image" button before analysis.
Exception Flow of Events::
 [E1] In the case that the uploaded file is not an image, the system will report an error on the screen.
